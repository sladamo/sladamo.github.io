Carnival Cruise - Cozumel & Grand Cayman...AGAIN!

In March 2016, during my spring break from school, Frank, my brother Nic, and I will be going on yet another Carnival cruise! For Frank and I, this is our 4th cruise together, while for Nic, it's his first time outside of the country! We plan on driving down to Florida to get a road trip experience as well. While on the cruise, we plan on doing a certified shipwreck dive in Grand Cayman, our very first wreck dive. We are so pumped! In Cozumel, we're going to take my brother on a Discover Scuba excursion, so he can experience the wonders of the deep for the first time. I know he is also very excited to put his first passport to use!



Maine
