Aruba, Jamaica, ooh I wanna take ya!

There's something very special about the Caribbean. Frank and I have visited 9 islands there: Cozumel, Grand Cayman, Jamaica, Turks & Caicos, Bahamas, St. Thomas, Antigua, Barbados, Tortola. Surprisingly, they are all very different, but have a common tropical theme. You can't grow tired of the islands, no matter how hard you try :-). We visited all of these islands by cruise. Cruises are such a great way to vacation!

Carnival Cruise 2014 - Turks & Caicos and the Bahamas

In May 2014, we took our third cruise together, to celebrate our 1-year wedding anniversary. We became scuba certified on this trip, since we love diving, and wanted to go deeper than ever before. We dove "The Wall" in Turks & Caicos, an exhilerating, world-renowned dive feautring a wall of coral that drops down 7,000 feet. Fortunately, we only dove to 90 feet, which was plenty of excitement for us! We saw nurse sharks, sea turtles, baracudas, and plenty of colorful fish and coral.




Princess Cruise 2012 - St. Thomas, Barbados, Antigua, Tortola

In January 2012, we took our second cruise together, which Frank's family. We dove in St. Thomas, and did a beach day in Antigua. My favorite memory from this cruise was swinging from a rope off of a pirate ship into the ocean in Barbados. It was a Jolly Roger 1 tour

Hawaii - Our Honeymoon on Maui and Kauai

We had both never been to Hawaii before, so it was an easy choice when deciding where to Honeymoon. Two days after our wedding in May 2013, we departed for 2 weeks on Maui and Kauai. We did more adventure activity on Maui, and more relaxation on Kauai, which was a perfect balance for a perfect trip. It is a very special place, and Chicago could use some serious Aloha!

  • Here we are exploring the famous Napali Coast by boat on Kauai. It was the last night of our Honeymoon before heading back to Chicago. After all this beauty, we sure could have used one more day!
